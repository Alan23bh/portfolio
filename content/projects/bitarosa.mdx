### This project focuses primarily on building a clear service-based website that helps a small business owner promote multiple services while driving new customer inquiries.

## Key Features

• Multi-service website for hair braiding, nail services, and event planning  
• Clear service sections with dedicated call-to-action buttons  
• Lead generation forms using WPForms  
• Mobile-friendly responsive layout  
• Testimonial carousel to build customer trust  
• Instagram integration for social proof and visual content

---

## Overview

BitaRosa.com is a service-based website built for a real client who operates
three businesses: hair braiding, nail services, and event planning. The goal of
the project was to create a simple but polished website that could clearly
communicate the services offered while making it easy for potential customers
to inquire or book.

The site acts as a central online presence for the business and supports lead
generation by directing visitors toward contact forms and social media.

---

## Problem

The client previously relied heavily on word-of-mouth and social media to find
new customers. Without a dedicated website, potential clients had limited
information about services, pricing, or how to contact the business.

Additionally, since the client offers multiple services, it was important to
organize the information in a way that clearly separates each offering without
making the site confusing.

---

## Solution

I built a WordPress-based website using the Astra theme and Gutenberg editor to
create a lightweight, flexible layout that highlights each service category.

The site was designed with a clear structure so users can quickly understand
the different services offered and easily contact the business through lead
forms or social media.

---

## What I built

Key elements of the site include:

• Dedicated service sections for hair braiding, nail services, and event
planning  
• Landing page layout optimized for clear service discovery  
• WPForms integration to capture customer inquiries  
• Testimonial section to build credibility and trust  
• Instagram integration to showcase recent work and visual portfolio  
• Mobile-responsive layout for users browsing from phones

I also manage the website on an ongoing basis, handling updates, small design
improvements, and monitoring performance.

---

## Technical Challenges

One challenge during development was an issue with the testimonial carousel not
displaying correctly on the live site due to caching conflicts. After
investigating the issue, I resolved it by adjusting the caching configuration so
the dynamic content would render properly.

This experience helped reinforce how caching can affect dynamic components in
WordPress environments.

---

## Results

Since launching the site, the business has seen an estimated **30% increase in
new customer inquiries**, largely due to the improved visibility and easier
contact process provided by the website.

The site now serves as a central hub where potential customers can quickly
understand the services offered and reach out directly.

---

## Lessons learned

Working with a real client helped me better understand how important clear
communication and simple user flows are for service-based websites. Rather than
focusing purely on technical complexity, the project emphasized designing an
experience that makes it easy for users to find information and contact the
business.

It also gave me more hands-on experience managing a production website,
handling small issues like caching conflicts, and maintaining the site after
launch.
